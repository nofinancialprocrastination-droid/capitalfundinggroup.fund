FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We work with a wide range of credit profiles. For MCAs and short-term loans, we can often work with scores as low as 500. Term loans and CRE financing typically require 620+. We evaluate the overall health of your business, not just your credit score.
Most business loan approvals are issued within 24–48 hours of receiving a complete application. Commercial real estate loans typically take 2–4 weeks depending on the complexity of the transaction and third-party appraisals.
For most business loans, we need 3–6 months of bank statements, a completed application, and a valid government ID. Larger or more complex deals (CRE, large term loans) may require tax returns, financial statements, and entity documents.
No. Our initial quote and pre-qualification process uses only a soft pull, which does not affect your credit score. A hard inquiry is only made at the final approval stage with your explicit consent.
We fund businesses and commercial properties in all 50 states. We are headquartered in Illinois and have particular expertise in the Midwest market, but serve clients nationwide.
Most of our loan products carry no prepayment penalties. Some longer-term structured deals or CRE products may have a yield-maintenance provision. Your advisor will disclose all terms clearly upfront before you sign anything.
For business loans, we fund up to $5 million. For commercial real estate, we handle transactions from $250,000 to $20 million+. Contact us for deals outside these ranges — we have lender relationships for special situations.
